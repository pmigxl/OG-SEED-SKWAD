TRIAL 1

1. Look for aruco marker by rotating robot up to 360 degrees 
2. Stop robot when aruco marker is found
3. Adjust for overshoot (Line up straight with marker)
4. Drive towards marker and stop within 1 foot
 
 TRIAL 2
 
1. Look for aruco marker by rotating robot up to 360 degrees 
2. Stop robot when aruco marker is found
3. Adjust for overshoot (Line up straight with marker)
4. Drive towards marker 
5. Stop so that the center of rotation of the robot lies at the marker position
// Marker MUST be between the wheels of the robot when robot stops
